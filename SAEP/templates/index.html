Course categories

Business

Balanced approach to the theory and application of business concepts, with attention to the knowledge and skills necessary for student success in this course and beyond.

Health & Psychology

Courses are ideal for health and social care professionals who would like to learn more about applied psychology as well as different medical fields.

Accounting

Courses in accounting helps the decision-makers of a company to make effective choices, by providing information on the financial status of the business.

Science & Technology

As a broad field, science and technology courses cover a breadth of disciplines, including biotechnology, life and physical science, animal and plant science and information technology.

Art & Media

Courses in Art provides a foundation of visual arts knowledge and skills and their connections to other subjects, and explores the discipline in depth both intellectually and experientially.

Real Estate

Real estate course could have the skills and education required for a position as a property funding specialist, property valuer, property analyst, property development advisor or land economist.

Language

You are learning languages online in order to study or work abroad or simply want to explore different cultures, you will find courses to help you on your way.

Media

Media courses introduces students to the study of contemporary forms of mediated forms ofcommunication. Issues include: the economics of media production; the impact of media on individual attitudes, values and behaviors; the role of media professionals etc.
